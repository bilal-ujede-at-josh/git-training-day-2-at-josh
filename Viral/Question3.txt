Q3. How to combine two commits into one commit? For Example, you have 4 different commits C1, C2, C3
, and  C4 and you to combine C2 and C3 into one single commit. 

for combining multiple commit we have to open rebase interactive shell
in which we have to pick commits 
and we have we squash the commit which we want to combine.

$$$ git rebase -i HEAD~4

1. pick c1
2. pick c2
3. squash c3
4. pick c4
